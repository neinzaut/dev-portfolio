my projects

Talk2DHand

A collaborative output for our courses entitled Machine Learning and Natural Language Processing which aims to make ASL education more accessible and inclusive. The application is split into Learning, Practice, and Self-Test, taking audio and video as its input.

DocuMed

A collaborative output for our courses entitled Software Engineering and Algorithms and Complexity. DocuMed is a clinic management system catered towards De La Salle University - Dasmarinas' East and West Clinic. Its features include an inventory management, appointment scheduling, and record keeping.

Cafeium

An entry to De La Salle University - Dasmarinas' FRESH START 2024 Competition organized by the University Student Government, which won First Place. Cafeium is a gamified habit-building tracker wherein users can maintain their cafe through accomplishing tasks on their to-do lists, adding onto their daily journal entry, or by interacting with their peers.

AgriKado

An entry to Old St. Labs' C(old) (St)art Hackathon 2024 which won Third Place out of 12 teams. Agrikado is an e-commerce application that aims to promote local products while at the same time addressing climate change, particularly with how a tree is planted through each purchase made on the platform.

Move In Colour

A collaborative output for KadaKareer's UI/UX Challenge, which won Kollab's Choice. The task was to create a website for Move In Colour, a non-profit organization that aims to raise awareness and take action to create a world where everyone can move, play and dream without boundaries.

MedGrocer

A collaborative output for KadaKareer's UI/UX Challenge. The task was to redesign MedGrocer's website, which is an online pharmateutical company who also has other services such as clinic management services, and patient support programs.
