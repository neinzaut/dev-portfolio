Talk2DHand

An undergraduate thesis project which aims to make sign language education more accessible and inclusive. The current MVP is split into Learning, Practice, and Self-Test, taking audio and video as its input. Enhancements are in progress, consisting of widening the language scope and adjusting it to a gamified LMS platform.
